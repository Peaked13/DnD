Record of the Roles and Ranks of the Servants of Dread Asmodeus, Archfiend, Lord of the Ninth
Spell Book containing:
	Lesser Planar Binding (6)
	Planar Binding (7)
	Greater Planar Binding (8)
	Gate (9)
Contains the true names of numerous devils, granting the ability to summon those specific devils via the spells above and similar as well as giving a +5 on charisma ability and skill checks with those devils (only for those who can read them in Infernal).
